What is Sextortion?

Sextortion is a form of blackmail where someone threatens to share intimate images or recordings unless demands are met.

Step 1

Perpetrator obtains intimate content.

Step 2

Threats to expose content publicly.

Step 3

Demands payment, images, or other favors.

Protect Yourself

  • Never share intimate images with strangers.
  • Use strong privacy settings.
  • Enable two-factor authentication.
  • Be wary of unsolicited requests.

If You’re a Victim

  1. Do NOT pay or comply.
  2. Preserve evidence (screenshots, messages).
  3. Report to law enforcement.
  4. Contact platform support.
  5. Seek trusted support (friends, counselors).
